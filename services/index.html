WHAT I OFFER

As mentioned on the About Me page, I want to offer you an intervention style out of the ordinary. Every session lasts 55 minutes. You can talk to me over the phone or on Teams. All conversations are being recorded to ensure the quality of my work and for future reference.

I can guarantee you that after each conversation, you will have a take away that will help you face, and if possible, change what has led you to contact me. In your own words, you must be able to summarize your situation in about 10 minutes. Your words are very important as they describe your reality. If these criteria cannot be met, we might have to speak again.

In any case, I want to help you in the best way possible and as fast as possible. I aim for your independence and your empowerment. If you need more than one session, we will work on an intervention plan together.

If you are doubting the virtues of a single-session intervention, I salute your critical thinking and I would refer you to the FAQ page where I address the question.

I am a versatile professional. I can intervene with couples, families, organizations or businesses. Please email me to explain your situation. We will then discuss the objectives and the number of sessions needed.

I can also provide advice to professionals working in the care business. This is not meant to be a clinical supervision but an opinion from one professional to another. If you are going through a clinical standstill, I am able to help.
